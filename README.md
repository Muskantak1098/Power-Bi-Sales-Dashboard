#Power BI Sales Dashboards

Two interactive Power BI dashboards showcasing ecommerce and retail sales analytics with rich visualizations and business insights.

## Dashboard 1 — Shri Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

### Overview
A comprehensive ecommerce sales analytics dashboard tracking profit, quantity, revenue, and customer behavior across different states, categories, and payment modes in India.

### Key KPIs

| Metric | Value |

|  Total Profit | ₹37K |
|  Total Quantity Sold | 6K Units |
|  Average Order Value (AOV) | ₹121+ |
|  Total Revenue (Amount) | ₹438K |

### Key Insights
- **Printers** is the highest profit generating sub-category
- **Clothing** dominates with 62.62% of total quantity sold
- **Maharashtra** is the top revenue generating state
- **COD** is the most preferred payment method at 43.74%
- Profit peaks during **festive seasons** (Oct-Nov, Jan)
- Mid-year (May–Aug) shows a consistent profit dip

## Dashboard 2 — Sales Dashboard 2026

###  Overview
A modern global retail sales dashboard tracking product performance and category-wise revenue from Jan 2024 to Apr 2025.

### Key KPIs

| Metric | Value |
| 👥 Total Customers | 12 |
| 📋 Total Orders | 39 |
| 💰 Total Sales | $33.00K |

###  Key Insights
- **Laptops** contribute 44.9% of total revenue
- **Dell XPS 15** is the top selling product at $7,800
- **iPad Air 6** is a close second at $7,600
- Sales peaked in mid-2024, followed by a dip in Q4
- Dashboard covers 4 countries: China, Germany, India, United States
